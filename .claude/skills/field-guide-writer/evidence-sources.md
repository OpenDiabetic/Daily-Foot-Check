# Field Guide evidence sources (tiered)
## Tier A — anchor here
- IWGDF Guidelines 2023 (iwgdfguidelines.org) — prevention, practical,
  classification, footwear/offloading, wound, PAD, infection chapters.
  Risk stratification 0–3 is the organizing model.
- ADA Standards of Care in Diabetes (current year) — foot-care section.
## Tier B — supporting
- NICE NG19 (diabetic foot problems), national podiatry bodies.

## Scope guardrail
Self-care education ONLY. Clinician-scope topics (debridement, offloading
device prescription, wound dressing selection, antibiotic guidance) are
OUT OF BOUNDS — reference "your care team" instead.
## Freshness rule
Guidelines are versioned; re-verify the current IWGDF edition at draft time
and record the year. Never cite from memory.
